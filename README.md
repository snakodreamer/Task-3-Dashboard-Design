# Task-3-Dashboard-Design
This project was completed as part of a Data Analyst Internship assessment. The objective was to design an interactive business dashboard using Microsoft Power BI that enables stakeholders to monitor sales performance, analyse trends, and make data-driven decisions.
The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful visual insights through interactive charts, KPIs, and filters.
Dataset Used: Superstore Sales Dataset

The dataset contains transactional sales information, including:
•	Order Date
•	Sales
•	Profit
•	Quantity
•	Customer Segment
•	Product Category
•	Region
•	State
•	Discount
•	Ship Mode

Tools Used
•	Microsoft Power BI Desktop
•	GitHub

The dashboard includes:
•	📈 Profit by State
•	📊 Sales by Product Category
•	🥧 Sales Distribution by Customer Segment
•	📉 Sum of Quantity by Order ID
• 📉 Sum of Profit by Year and Category
•	📋 KPI Cards
o	Total Sales
o	Total Profit
o	Profit Margin %
o Total Orders
o	Quantity Sold

Key Insights
•	Sales fluctuate throughout the year, indicating seasonal purchasing patterns.
•	Certain product categories contribute significantly more revenue than others.
•	Customer segments differ in their contribution to total sales.
•	Regional performance varies, highlighting opportunities for targeted business strategies.
•	Interactive filtering allows stakeholders to analyse performance across different regions, categories, and customer segments.

Business Recommendations
•	Increase investment in high-performing product categories.
•	Improve marketing efforts in underperforming regions.
•	Review pricing and discount strategies to maximise profitability.
•	Prepare inventory for periods of high seasonal demand.
•	Continue monitoring KPIs to support strategic decision-making.
